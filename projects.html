Current Research Projects

LLM-Based Coding of SRL Think-Aloud Protocols

Automated coding frameworks that use large language models to code think-aloud protocol data on self-regulated learning, developed to preserve theoretical fidelity while scaling qualitative analysis.

Invited Revision · Journal of Learning Analytics

LLM Feedback for Teacher Professional Vision

An international collaboration with the University of Tübingen (INFER Project) evaluating how prompting strategies shape LLM-generated feedback and pre-service teacher engagement with it.

Accepted · LAK '26

AI-Based Self-Regulated Learning Chatbot

Design of an AI-based chatbot for STEM learners built around autonomy, competence, and relatedness, aimed at promoting self-regulated learning through conversational support.

Under Review · IJETHE

Causal Moderation with Continuous Moderators

A double-weighting approach for estimating causal moderation effects in observational studies with binary treatments and continuous moderators, extending propensity-score methods for heterogeneous treatment effects.

Invited Revision · JEBS

Digital Self-Regulation Interventions in STEM

Evaluating intervention effects on SRL behaviors and motivation among community college and university STEM students, and using survival analysis to model dropout risk from early course performance.

Learning Analytics Center, UNC

Resource-Intervention Match Framework

A framework explaining why self-regulated learning interventions have differential effects across learners, by modeling the match between learner resources and intervention design.

Published · Learning and Individual Differences (2026)
