About Me

Hi there! I'm a passionate researcher exploring the fascinating intersection of self-regulated learning, AI technologies, and educational data science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

My research journey combines rigorous methodological approaches with a deep commitment to improving educational experiences. I believe that by understanding how students learn and applying advanced analytics, we can create more personalized and effective learning environments for everyone.

My hobbies include reading scientific fiction and dancing. These activities help me maintain a creative mindset and work-life balance while pursuing my academic goals.
